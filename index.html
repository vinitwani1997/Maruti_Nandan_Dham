About Us

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2025, Maruti Nandan Dham began as a small temple with a mission to serve the community. What started as a place of worship soon transformed into a center for charitable activities, driven by the principles of seva (service) and bhakti (devotion).

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted organization, touching thousands of lives through our various initiatives in education, healthcare, and community development.

Our work is inspired by the teachings of Lord Hanuman, symbolizing strength, devotion, and selfless service.

Our Mission

To serve humanity with compassion and devotion, providing education, healthcare, and spiritual nourishment to the underprivileged sections of society.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities for spiritual growth, regardless of their economic status.

Our Values

Service, Compassion, Integrity, Devotion, and Community. These core values guide all our initiatives and decision-making processes.

Our Work

Education for All

We run free schools and tuition centers for underprivileged children, providing quality education, books, and uniforms to over 500 students annually.

Health Camps

Monthly health camps offering free medical check-ups, medicines, and awareness programs in rural areas, benefiting 200+ people each month.

Food Distribution

Daily annadanam (food donation) program serving nutritious meals to 300+ needy people, with special focus on elderly and children.

Women Empowerment

Vocational training programs for women including tailoring, handicrafts, and computer skills, helping them become financially independent.

Spiritual Activities

Regular satsangs, bhajans, and spiritual discourses to nourish the soul and promote values of peace, harmony, and service.

Disaster Relief

Providing immediate relief during natural calamities including food, clothing, medicines, and temporary shelter to affected communities.
